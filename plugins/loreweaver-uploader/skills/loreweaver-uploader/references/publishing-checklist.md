# Hub publication checklist

Before publishing, verify against the current live UI and current Community Guidelines:

- title, description, tags, and content rating accurately represent the experience;
- the cover complies with cover-image standards;
- the creator owns or has permission to share text and images;
- no private notes, hidden instructions, personal data, or unrelated conversation content are exposed;
- world premise, style rules, lore cards, and public metadata contain no prohibited material;
- AI-generated content has been reviewed by the creator;
- public visibility and any download/remix/comment settings are intentional;
- a playtest has exercised the opening, key lore retrieval, and every critical Loom transition;
- update diffs contain no accidental deletion or visibility change.

Public publication always requires explicit action-time confirmation.
